Egg Hoppers

Traditional Sri Lankan dish made with fermented rice batter and coconut milk. These bowl-shaped crepes are known for their crispy edges and soft, spongy centers, making them a delightful treat for breakfast or brunch.

A rich and creamy Sri Lankan dessert pudding made with coconut milk and jaggery. Watalappan is infused with spices like cardamom and nutmeg, creating a luscious and indulgent treat for sweet enthusiasts.
